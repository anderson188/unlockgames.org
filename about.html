About unLockGames

Editorial shopping guides, transparent deals & affiliate partnerships

Who We Are

unLockGames (unlockgames.org) is an English-language consumer website. We publish original and editorial-style buying guides, category roundups, and practical tips for shoppers—primarily in electronics & tech, fashion & beauty, sport & activewear, travel, and general retail. Our audience is international: readers searching in English for trustworthy product and merchant information before they buy.

What We Publish

Buying guides & brand-focused articles: We explain what to look for, compare options, and help readers make informed choices. Content is written for real purchase intent, not keyword stuffing.

Deals & offers: We highlight promotions and savings opportunities where they are relevant to our readers. Offer details can change; we encourage users to confirm terms on the merchant’s official site at checkout.

On-site tools: We provide an on-site AI chat tool for general questions. It is separate from our commerce editorial content and is not used to disguise paid promotion.

Our Mission

We aim to help readers save time and money with clear, honest information—while respecting brand guidelines and the rules of the affiliate programmes we join. We do not claim to represent any brand unless explicitly stated.

How We Monetise (Affiliate Disclosure)

unLockGames participates in affiliate marketing programmes (including networks such as Awin and others). When you click certain links and complete a qualifying purchase, we may earn a commission or referral fee at no extra cost to you. Not all links are affiliate links; where required, we disclose relationships near the content or in our Privacy and Terms pages.

We only promote merchants and offers in line with each programme’s terms. We use approved creatives and tracking when a partner requires it, and we do not encourage invalid clicks, misleading claims, or trademark abuse.

Editorial Standards

We strive for accurate, up-to-date information. Merchant policies, prices, and stock change frequently—readers should always verify on the official retailer site. If you spot an error, please contact us; we will correct material issues when we can.
