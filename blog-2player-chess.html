2Player Online Chess Guide

Introduction to Chess

Chess is a classic strategy game played between two players on a checkered board. Our online version allows you to play against friends or the computer, with real-time gameplay and intuitive controls.

How to Play

  1. Set up the board with white pieces on one side and black pieces on the other
  2. White always moves first
  3. Take turns moving one piece at a time
  4. Capture opponent's pieces by moving to their square
  5. Checkmate the opponent's king to win

Basic Strategies

  • Control the center of the board
  • Develop your pieces early
  • Protect your king by castling
  • Look for forks, pins, and skewers
  • Plan several moves ahead
