How to Play

  1. Set up the board with white pieces on one side and black pieces on the other
  2. White always moves first
  3. Take turns moving one piece at a time
  4. Capture opponent's pieces by moving to their square
  5. Checkmate the opponent's king to win

Opening Principles

  • Control the center
  • Develop minor pieces
  • Castle early

Middle Game

  • Look for tactical opportunities
  • Coordinate your pieces
  • Plan your strategy

Endgame

  • Activate your king
  • Promote pawns
  • Know basic checkmates
