Chicken Scream Game Guide

About the Game

Chicken Scream is a hilarious physics-based game where you help a chicken escape from the farm. Use your voice or mouse clicks to make the chicken scream and propel it through various obstacles and challenges.

Gameplay Video

Tips & Tricks

  • Time your screams carefully to maintain momentum
  • Use short bursts for precision movements
  • Collect coins to unlock new chicken skins
  • Watch out for obstacles like fences and farm equipment
