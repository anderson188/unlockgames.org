Make America Great Again Game Guide

About the Game

Make America Great Again is a political strategy game where you manage a presidential campaign. Make key decisions, manage resources, and win the election!

Gameplay Tips

Basic Strategies

  • Balance your campaign funds between advertising and events
  • Choose policies that appeal to your target demographics
  • Manage your candidate's energy and public image

Advanced Tactics

  • Time your campaign events for maximum impact
  • Respond strategically to opponent attacks
  • Use data analytics to target key voter groups
