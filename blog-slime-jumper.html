Slime Jumper Game Guide

About the Game

Slime Jumper is an exciting platformer game where you control a cute slime character. Jump through challenging levels, collect coins, and avoid obstacles to reach the finish line!

Gameplay Tips

Basic Controls

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move
  • Spacebar to jump
  • Hold spacebar for higher jumps

Advanced Techniques

  • Wall jumping: Bounce off walls to reach higher platforms
  • Combo jumps: Chain multiple jumps for extra height
  • Power-ups: Collect special items for temporary boosts
