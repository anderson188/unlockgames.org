How to Play

In SPRUNKI 3D Escape, your goal is to navigate through various 3D environments and find your way to freedom. Here's how to play:

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  2. Mouse or touchpad to look around the environment
  3. Space bar to jump
  4. Interact with objects using the E key
  5. Solve puzzles to progress through levels
  6. Find keys and unlock doors to reach new areas

Tips and Strategies

  • Always explore your surroundings thoroughly - hidden clues might be anywhere
  • Pay attention to environmental hints that might help solve puzzles
  • If you get stuck, try looking at the problem from a different angle
  • Remember the patterns you discover - they might be useful later
  • Take your time with puzzles - rushing might make you miss important details
