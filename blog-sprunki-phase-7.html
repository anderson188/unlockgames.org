How to Play

Master these controls to conquer Sprunki Phase 7:

  1. WASD or arrow keys for movement
  2. Space bar for basic jump
  3. Double tap space for double jump
  4. Shift key for special abilities (when available)
  5. Mouse to adjust camera view
  6. E key to interact with objects

Advanced Techniques

  • Wall jumping for reaching high places
  • Speed running techniques
