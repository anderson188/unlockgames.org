Tiny Fishing

Game Introduction

Tiny Fishing is a relaxing and fun fishing simulation game where players can catch various fish species, upgrade their equipment, and explore new fishing spots.

Gameplay Guide

  1. Tap to cast your fishing line
  2. Quickly tap to reel in when fish bite
  3. Control the tension to avoid line break
  4. Catch rare fish for more coins
  5. Use coins to upgrade gear and buy bait

Gameplay Video

Pro Tips

  • Different baits work best for different fish
  • Upgrade your rod to improve fishing efficiency
  • Watch for water ripples to locate fish
  • Complete daily tasks for extra rewards
  • Explore new areas to discover rare fish
