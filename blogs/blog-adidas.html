Adidas: From Bavarian Workshop to Global Sportswear Empire

Sportswear · Adidas · March 2025

The Birth of the Three Stripes

In 1924, in a small town in Bavaria, Germany, Adolf "Adi" Dassler began crafting athletic shoes in his mother's washroom. His obsession with helping athletes perform better led to innovations like the first spiked running shoe. By 1949, Adidas was officially born—and the three stripes became one of the most recognizable logos in the world.

Adidas Today: Innovation Meets Heritage

Adidas has outfitted Olympic champions, World Cup winners, and everyday athletes for decades. The brand's commitment to sustainability—with initiatives like Primeblue (ocean plastic) and Futurecraft.Loop (fully recyclable shoes)—shows they're thinking beyond the next season. Collaborations with Stella McCartney, Beyoncé's IVY PARK, and Yeezy (historically) have kept Adidas at the forefront of culture and fashion.

Latest Drops: What's Hot Right Now

The Ultraboost 5 continues to dominate the running scene with its responsive cushioning. For lifestyle, the Samba and Gazelle have made a massive comeback. And if you're into basketball, the Harden Vol. 8 brings performance tech to the court. Whatever your sport or style, Adidas has a wide range to explore.
