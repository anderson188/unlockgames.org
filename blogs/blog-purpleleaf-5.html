How to Choose Purple Leaf Pergola Size and Color

2026-05-05 · Outdoor Furniture

Pergola shopping becomes easier when you decide size first, then color second. Most buyers do the reverse and end up with a beautiful frame that feels oversized or too tight.

Size by furniture footprint

Measure the largest furniture arrangement you want underneath, then add side clearance for movement and cleaning. This gives a realistic minimum pergola dimension.

Match color to walls and flooring

Warm tones pair well with wood decks and beige walls. Charcoal and grey options look cleaner against modern concrete, black frames, and neutral cushions.

Check installation context early

Before ordering, confirm local installation rules, drainage direction, and any HOA requirements. Solving these details first avoids delays and redesign costs.
