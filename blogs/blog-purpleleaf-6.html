Purple Leaf Outdoor Furniture Maintenance Calendar

2026-05-05 · Outdoor Furniture

Outdoor furniture lasts longer when maintenance is scheduled instead of reactive. A simple monthly routine is enough for most households and prevents expensive replacement cycles.

Monthly check: fabric and fasteners

Inspect canopy fabric for wear, clean visible dust, and tighten loose hardware. Early fixes are cheaper than replacing full components later.

Season change check: frame and drainage

At each season change, review frame alignment and drainage paths around your setup. Water pooling is one of the most common causes of premature wear.

Before storm periods

Secure movable parts, confirm base stability, and store loose cushions. Preventive prep takes minutes and reduces storm-related damage risk.
