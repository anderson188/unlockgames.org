Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to unLockGames ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform for cashback deals, coupons, buying guides, and free online games.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, links clicked, and time spent on our site—including when you browse deals, coupons, or articles
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • IP address and approximate location: For analytics and security purposes
  • Cookies and similar technologies: To remember preferences and track affiliate referrals
  • Gameplay data: When you play games on our site, third-party game providers may collect session data
  • AI Assistant usage: When you use our AI Assistant (chat), the messages you send and the AI’s replies are processed by our AI provider to generate responses. We do not store your chat history on our servers; the provider may process and retain data according to its own policy (see Section 4).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services—deals, coupons, buying guides, and games
  • Track affiliate referrals when you shop through our links (required for cashback)
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns to improve content and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We work with third parties that may collect or process your data:

  • Retailers and affiliate networks: When you click our coupon or cashback links, you are directed to partner sites with their own privacy policies
  • Game distribution platforms: Free games are hosted by third parties who may collect gameplay data
  • AI / LLM providers: Our AI Assistant sends your chat messages to a third-party AI service to generate replies. That provider may process, log, or retain your inputs and responses under its own privacy policy. We do not control how the provider uses this data. If you prefer not to have your questions processed by an AI provider, do not use the AI Assistant.
  • Analytics services: We use analytics to understand how visitors use our site
  • Advertising networks: We may display ads; these networks have their own privacy policies

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit through our links.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Track affiliate referrals for cashback and commission purposes
  • Analyze traffic and improve our site

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect affiliate tracking and your ability to earn cashback through our links.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Affiliate and cashback partners (to attribute purchases and process rewards)
  • Service providers who assist our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also opt out of certain analytics or advertising. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.
